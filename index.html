What we do

Three services. One clear order.

The community is the destination. YouTube brings people to it. Social media supports both. Here is exactly what each one includes, in plain words.

Step 1

Your Skool community, built from zero

This is the heart of everything. A Skool community is where your followers become paying members. We build it completely, so you never touch a single setting. It can be free or paid, and if you're not sure which fits your goals, we help you decide on our first call.

Complete setup & branding

Your community created from zero: banner design, branding, and every membership and access setting configured properly.

Classroom built page by page

We structure your classroom sections in the right learning order, build your Start Here section with a welcome, roadmap, rules, and FAQ, organize your resource vault, and write the content for every page.

Pricing strategy

For paid communities we set your launch price, plan when to raise it, and structure your tiers, so early members feel urgency and you never undersell yourself.

All launch copywriting

The community description people read before joining, your launch video script, announcement messages for WhatsApp and email, and your first community post. All written for you.

Landing page & funnel

A custom landing page for your community, a webinar structure for converting leads, and a full email sequence covering welcome, follow-up, and urgency. The complete path from stranger to member.

First month of content, included

After launch we post in your community daily, welcome every new member, and reply to comments and messages for the entire first month at no extra charge.

Step 2

Your YouTube channel, launched & optimized

A community with no traffic stays empty. YouTube is the single best way community owners bring in new members, because every video naturally points viewers toward your community. No channel yet? We launch one. Already have one? We optimize it.

Channel setup from zero

Channel branding, banner design, and a complete about section, so your channel looks professional from the very first day.

Content strategy & first videos

We research your niche, define your content pillars, and hand you your first 8 to 12 video topics, each one keyword-researched so it has a real chance to rank.

Thumbnails & SEO

We design thumbnails for your first 8 to 12 videos and give you a proven title and description formula. Every video you upload gets keyword research, an optimized title, and an optimized description.

Editing & uploading

You film, we handle the rest: editing, thumbnail, SEO, and publishing on schedule. Your only job is recording.

Step 3

Social media & everything around it

Instagram, X, Facebook, and LinkedIn all serve one purpose in our system: sending people toward your YouTube and your community. We set up every account from zero, or take over your existing ones, and keep them active so you stay visible everywhere without managing anything.

All accounts set up from zero

Instagram, X, Facebook, and LinkedIn: created, branded, and configured to match your community. Already have accounts? We optimize them instead.

3 to 4 posts per week, per platform

Tips, value posts, and content related to AI and your niche, written and published for you every week on every platform, all promoting your community.

Short-form video topics

Every week you receive 2 to 3 researched short-video topics. You record once, and the same clip works for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, X, and LinkedIn.

Email campaigns

Consistent email campaigns to your list: announcements, webinar invitations, and reminders, so every subscriber keeps hearing about your community.

FAQ

Common questions

Can you help with a free community, or only paid?

Both. A free community grows your audience and builds trust. A paid community turns that audience into monthly income. Many creators start free and launch a paid tier later. We build either one, and help you choose on our first call.

I already run a community. Can you just manage and grow it?

Yes. You skip the setup fee and start directly on the $500 per month Growth Retainer. We take over daily posting, onboarding, retention, and social promotion, and send you a monthly report.

What decides where I fall in the $800 to $1,500 range?

Scope. Community-only work (setup, classroom, copy, landing page) sits at the lower end. Adding the full YouTube launch and all four social platforms from zero moves toward the top. Your free audit report includes one fixed quote before we start, so there are no surprises.

How does the monthly retainer work?

Your setup fee covers the entire build plus your first month of daily management at no extra cost. From month two, the Growth Retainer continues at $500 per month, covering daily posting, member onboarding, retention systems, social media, email campaigns, 1-on-1 support, and your monthly performance report. It keeps your community growing without taking a single hour of your week.

What do you need from me?

Your expertise and your face. You know your topic, and only you can record your videos. We handle structure, writing, design, posting, editing, and management. Expect one strategy call and quick feedback rounds during the build.

How fast can we launch?

About 30 days from the strategy call to a live, launched community, with your YouTube and social accounts running alongside it.
